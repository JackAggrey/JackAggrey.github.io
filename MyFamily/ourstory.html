Our Story

The journey of our live started in Novermber, 2013 when I meet her coming from work. We started dating but the unfortunate thing was that, I was living in Kumasi while she was in Takoradi.

We dated for six months and got married in April 1st, 2014. It was a lovely occasion because that was a Easter monday and April Fool day as well. But all the same the church full to it capacity. My mate from University of Education-Winneba, Kumasi branch came in their number as well as her friend from her workplace and her mate in Takoradi Ploytachnic all came.

We have been married for Five years now and God has blessed us with two lovely girls; Maame Adwoa and Ewurabena. Though, there has been ups and downs for this five years but God is in control.

Maame Adwoa

Maame was born in Novermber 17th, 2014. She is a girl everybody wishes to be with. She likes asking questions and wants to do things on her own sometime.

Ewurabena

My second girl, she was born in Novermber 1st, 2016, she is quiet and lovely girl. Alway want to be closer to any one of us when we are around.
